About us.

We are a group of students in Birmingham with one goal; making sure your student years are the best they can be.

We have been to all the clubs Birmingham has to offer, and created a guide to help you find the perfect spot for your nights out. Whatever your music taste, vibe and interests are we can guarantee Birmingham has something to offer. We are just simply here to help you find that place.

And don't worry, as fellow students we know money is tight. We wanted to create a place where you can find the best clubs on a budget. Including drink deals, cheap entry and events.

We always aim to improve, if you have any queries or feedback, please go to the Contact Us page, and we will do our best to help you!
